# [샘플 학습자료] 중학교 1학년 영어 — Lesson 4 단어와 표현

> 단원 어휘 정리

## 핵심 단어

| 단어 | 뜻 | 예문 |
|---|---|---|
| favorite | 가장 좋아하는 | What's your favorite subject? |
| subject | 과목 | My favorite subject is science. |
| practice | 연습하다 | I practice the piano every day. |
| join | 가입하다, 함께하다 | Why don't you join our club? |
| exercise | 운동하다; 운동 | I exercise three times a week. |
| healthy | 건강한 | Eating vegetables is healthy. |
| habit | 습관 | Getting up early is a good habit. |
| weekend | 주말 | What do you do on weekends? |
| usually | 보통, 대개 | I usually walk to school. |
| together | 함께 | Let's study together. |

## 핵심 표현

1. **What's your favorite ~?** : 가장 좋아하는 ~이 뭐니?
   - A: What's your favorite food? / B: My favorite food is pizza.
2. **Why don't you ~?** : ~하는 게 어때? (제안)
   - Why don't you join the book club?
3. **How often do you ~?** : 얼마나 자주 ~하니?
   - A: How often do you exercise? / B: Three times a week.

## 확인 문제

1. '습관'을 뜻하는 단어는? → habit
2. "I ( ) the piano every day." 빈칸에 알맞은 것은? → practice
3. 제안할 때 쓰는 표현 "( ) don't you join us?" → Why
4. usually의 뜻은? → 보통, 대개
